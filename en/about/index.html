Born and raised in Tokyo, Japan, Inoue has spent the past 18 years carefully developing a series called Silenzioso – Silent Prayer. Devoting all of his imagination he created 67 images of European scenery on hand-made sculptural paper. The results are rich with emotions.

Time passes with respect to the each moment. Depth allows one to wonder into every space and corners in all distance. And, the warmth of rice paper invites one into the valued breath and beats of life.
